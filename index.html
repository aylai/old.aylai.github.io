I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I am advised by Julia Hockenmaier.

My interests are in natural language processing and machine learning. My PhD research has focused on how to tackle semantic tasks like textual entailment using image caption denotations.

If you are looking for the Flickr30K image-caption corpus or our denotation graph data (as described in From image description to visual denotations, TACL 2014), please contact Julia Hockenmaier.
